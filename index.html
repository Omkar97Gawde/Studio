Get Started

We have grown with the voice-over and ADR Industry in India.

Having been in this Industry for over 3 decades, we have seen its evolution and have consistently contributed to its organisation and development. Today we stand as an undisputed market leader . We have in our repertoire 1000s of hours of language localisation projects and dubbed content.

  • Our clients are television channels , production houses, advertising agencies as well as corporates seeking the best quality in writing , voices and audio production. Our endeavour is ably supported by our own State of the art studios , competent and dedicated engineers , a vast network of voice and writing talent.

  • Subhash Studios is today a hub for Indian and Global TV channels as well as localisation agencies , for their dubbing requirements in Indian languages , including shows with music , songs and ditties. Our studios have been approved by channels like Discovery, Disney and subsidiaries of BBC. We strive for an output we are proud to present. Since we offer voicing solutions in all Indian Languages , we can offer a one stop solution to your dubbing , voicing and localisation requirements.

You don't need to look any further. Whether you need music, voice overs, translations, trans-creation or your own customised requirements; we have got the solution - all under one roof. Our team is here to help you put together the best talent at every step to give you world class output.
